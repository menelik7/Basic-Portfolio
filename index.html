About Me

I am originally from Ethiopia and have been living in the U.S. for 12 years. I currently work as a Project Engineer at Benchmark management LLC, mainly managing vertical construciton projects. My job is highly demanding, often-times stressful, and requires a good deal of time management. I enjoy what I do, but it does not offer opportunities to be genuinely creative.

I have been fascinated with software development dating back to the first time I was introduced to the Commodore 64. The manual included pre-written programs. One rendered miniature hot-air ballons flying across the screen in random directions, and the other was a segment of a musical keyboard. The rush when the code works is indescribable. The fantasy of getting there is even more addictive. I have finally surrendered to my passion.
